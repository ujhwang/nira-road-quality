Metro Atlanta Road Roughness & Anomaly Map

This interactive web map displays road roughness and recorded road anomalies across 21 counties in the Metro Atlanta region.

Road roughness is measured using the International Roughness Index (IRI) in inches per mile. The displayed value represents an aggregated IRI over the past 60 days, with more recent measurements weighted more heavily.

Anomalies include potholes, bumps, and other surface issues. A higher severity indicates a more significant anomaly, while the hit ratio represents the proportion of passing vehicles that detected the anomaly.

Click on road segments or anomaly points to view detailed information. Use the checkboxes to filter the data by road type or anomaly type.
